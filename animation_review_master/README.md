# Animation Review Master

Domain: `animation_review_master`

Encodes **structured critique judgment** — how to diagnose why animation feels wrong, what structural causes produce cheap/AI-looking/chaotic results, and how to fix them.

This domain covers:
- **Critique the path, not the effect**: Most bad animation is structurally broken, not aesthetically ugly
- **Cheapness has causes**: Every quality label (cheap, AI-looking, chaotic) maps to a specific structural problem
- **Review with viewer task**: Evaluate animation against the viewer's primary goal, not theory
- **Fix by removing first**: Most broken animations are over-animated; remove 30% and re-evaluate

Part of the open-source [Animation KDNA Cluster](../README.md).
