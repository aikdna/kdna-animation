# Motion Design Master

Domain: `motion_design_master`

Encodes **director-level motion judgment** — the principles that make animation feel intentional, premium, and communicative before any tool is chosen.

This domain covers:
- **Attention first**: Motion exists to control attention, not to decorate
- **Timing as meaning**: Duration and easing communicate confidence, urgency, elegance, or playfulness
- **Restraint creates premium**: Premium motion is precise, sparse, and intentional
- **Choreography over effects**: The sequence of events matters more than novelty

Part of the open-source [Animation KDNA Cluster](../README.md). Follows the six-file KDNA structure.
